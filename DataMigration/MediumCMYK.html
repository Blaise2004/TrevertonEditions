This first edition of Tryptic marked my introduction to CMYK screen printing and laid the technical foundation for the project. Drawing inspiration from the works of Paketa12, I explored glitch aesthetics through a recursive division process in TouchDesigner, fracturing the source image into block-like patterns. I then separated the work into CMYK layers in Photoshop, exporting each as an individual stencil for screen printing. The process produced unexpected overlaps and textures, with imperfections that lent the prints a tactile depth echoing their digital origins.

Only eight prints were produced, making this edition a rare prototype that set the tone for subsequent explorations.
